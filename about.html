Hello! I'm Caleb Flosky, a passionate Senior majoring in Computer Science at Michigan State University.

I'm currently enrolled in MI 497 within the MSU Game Design Minor, where I am developing a game called SpellForge as a Networking Gameplay Programmer focused on the spell system.

SpellForge is an online multiplayer top-down fantasy game built using Quantum 3, where 2 or 4 players explore a mystical village, collect powerful spells, and battle each other in fast-paced magical combat.

I love collaborating with fellow developers, building gameplay systems, and creating responsive, expressive experiences through code. Feel free to reach out — I'm always excited to connect!
