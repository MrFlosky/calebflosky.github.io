Goose Genie

Release Date: April 2024

My Role: Programming Director

Goose Genie is an upcoming game developed by Spartasoft Studio, currently in the development phase. As the Programming Director, I am one of the leaders over a club with 70 members, with a specific focus on managing the 20 programmers and facilitating collaboration with other disciplines. This single-player puzzle platformer revolves around a Goose trapped inside a teapot. The Goose is able to switch between different states of matter, each with its own set of unique attributes. Players will navigate through levels, solving puzzles and advancing through the game by using each state.

Sneak Easy

Released: April 25th, 2023

My Role: Player Programming & QTE Programming

"Sneak Easy" is a stealth platforming game that I helped develop as part of the programming team at Spartasoft Studio during Spring 2023. This captivating game emphasizes responsive character movement and Quick Time Events (QTEs), offering players an immersive experience as they navigate shadowy levels, outwitting adversaries with their agility and wits. My primary contributions focused on refining the character's movement and implementing exciting QTE moments, making "Sneak Easy" a thrilling adventure to move around in!

Battle Blobs

Released: March 26th, 2023

My Role: Player Programming & Painting Programming

Battle Blobs is a 2D fighting game created during Spartasoft Studio's March 2023 Game Jam in which the theme was: "MESSY". I was part of a six-person team, contributing as a programmer responsible for some character movement and the paint mechanic. In this fast-paced title, players engage in battle with the objective of covering the arena in paint. The player who claims the most paint-covered territory is winner. It served as an exciting test of our skills and creativity, as we aimed to see how far we could push the boundaries of game development within a mere 48 hours.

Share and Reach

Released: June 8th, 2021

My Role: Sole Developer: Programming, Design, Art, Audio

Share and Reach is a puzzle game designed for 1-2 players. In this game, you control two square-headed characters with distinct properties, working together to solve a variety of puzzles. Created as my final project in my C# and Game Design Year 2 course in 2021, I took on the role of the sole developer, responsible for programming, art, level design, and everything except the background music. Share and Reach delivers a puzzle-solving experience with its challenging gameplay.

Worlds Apart

Released: November 20, 2022

My Role: Animation, Character, Environmental Programmer

"Worlds Apart" was created during Spartasoft's November 2022 Game Jam, using the theme "Hello World" and bonus themes "Alchemy, Arcane, Potions." This 2D pixel game puts you in control of a stranded bot, crash-landed on a mysterious terrain. Your objective: establish your computer and uncover the hidden power source. Gameplay involves resource collection and crafting at a workbench. My role as a programmer centered on crafting mechanics, animations, character controls, and environmental elements.
